PBS Under Co-Payment Visualiser
Visualises if a drug is above or below the general co-payment amount for each month between Jan 2005 - July 2012.
Search for a drug name or ATC code to begin.

Caution!

1. Prices are displayed as the Dispensed Price for Maximum Quantity (DPMQ) - i.e. the maximum price for a given item code. The actual price paid may differ, but this should be broadly accurate.

2. Prices for dates prior to April 2007 may be inaccurate, as these had to be extracted from a worse quality data source :(.
